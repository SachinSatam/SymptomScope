Diabetes Prediction

  • Diabetes

    Diabetes is a chronic disease that occurs either when the pancreas does not produce

    enough insulin or when the body cannot effectively use the insulin it produces.

    Insulin is a hormone that regulates blood glucose.

    • Type 1

      Type 1 diabetes was previously known as insulin-dependent diabetes mellitus (IDDM)

      There is no cure for type 1 diabetes, but the condition can be well managed.
