    • Breast Cancer

      Breast cancer is a disease in which malignant (cancer)

      cells form in the tissues of the breast.

      • Ductal carcinoma

        Ductal carcinoma in situ (DCIS) is the presence

        of abnormal cells inside a milk duct in the breast.

        Physical
        - A breast lump
        - Redness of the skin of the breast
        - Persistent breast pain.

      • Invasive Ductal Carcinoma

        It is considered the earliest form of breast cancer.

        It is noninvasive, meaning it hasn't spread out of

        the milk duct and has a low risk of becoming invasive.

        Physical
        - Skin irritation
        - Nipple turning inward.
        - Persistent breast pain.

      • lobular carcinoma

        Needs mammograms tests.
